Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE)

Kochi Regional Centre

The Kochi Regional Centre of Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE), the premier body of professional chemical engineers in the country, made its presence in the year 1976 and has members from industry, statutory bodies and academia. The Regional Centre has been holding lectures, seminars and symposia covering wide range of topics like environment, health, safety, future technologies which are of relevance and interest to the society.Chemical Engineering profession, which has multi directional developments, today occupies a much wider canvas addressing problems concerning environment and ecology, biomedicine, energy and high performance materials.



The Kochi Regional Centre (previously known as Trichur Regional centre), established in 1976 is one among the 38 regional centres of IIChE. The centre has more than 250 members from industries and academic institutions located in the Kochi industrial belt and the northern districts of Kerala. The Regional Centre regularly organizes technical lectures, symposia, seminars and industrial visits for the benefit of its members. The Centre also promotes the activities of the four student chapters located within its jurisdiction
