Our Administration

The activities of the Kochi Regional Centre are coordinated by an executive committee comprising of elected members. The tenure of the committee is for an year. The functionaries of the committee comprise of the Chairman, the Vice- Chairman, the Honorary Regional Secretary, the Honorary Regional Joint Secretary and the Honorary Regional Treasurer.
